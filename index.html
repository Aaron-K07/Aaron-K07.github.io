PROJ-003 // NSS COMPETITION · FLAGSHIP★ 1ST PLACE — NSS 2025

ASTRAEUS II: Eden Garden Module

Co-developed an award-winning space agriculture habitat for long-duration human settlement, submitted to the NSS Live in a Healthy Space Design Competition. Led structural design and systems integration of a sustainable extraterrestrial agricultural module. Key innovations: magnetorheological fluid tilling system, AI-powered automated pollination vehicle, frequency-optimized plant growth (PAFT system), and integrated hydroponic/aeroponic/soil-based farming — achieving near 100% space efficiency and capacity for up to 592,391 inhabitants per settlement.
